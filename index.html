FAQ

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How do I redeem my cash back?+

Two ways: (1) Apply toward any merchant's digital gift card at checkout, or (2) request a check mailed to your address. Allow 5-7 business days for checks. Requests under $25 have a $5 processing fee.

Isn't this just like Rakuten?+

No. TapNPay+ works in-store AND online, at 100s of restaurants, and cash back is instant — not quarterly. Plus, TapNPay+ can be stacked with Rakuten for double rewards.

Can I use TapNPay+ with sales and coupons?+

Yes! Use coupons, promo codes, and store sales first, then pay with a TapNPay+ digital gift card to earn cash back on the already-discounted price.

Can I have multiple payment methods?+

Yes. Store multiple payment methods in your profile. You can nickname cards for easy reference.

How do I check a gift card balance?+

Go to your Wallet and press "Check Balance" next to any digital gift card.

Can I buy plastic gift cards with TapNPay+?+

Generally no. Most merchants don't allow digital gift cards to purchase physical gift cards.

Forgot to use TapNPay+ — can I still get cash back?+

Unfortunately not. Cash back only applies when digital gift cards are purchased through TapNPay+.
