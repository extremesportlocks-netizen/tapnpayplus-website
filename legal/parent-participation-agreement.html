PARENT PARTICIPATION AGREEMENT

AGREEMENT PARTIES

Company: TapNPay+, a registered trade name (DBA) of IntrActvMrktg, LLC, a Pennsylvania limited liability company. www.tapnpayplus.com · compliance@tapnpayplus.com

Parent: The parent, legal guardian, or authorized adult caregiver identified in the registration profile completed at the time of agreement execution.

Student: The K-12 student named as the beneficiary in the registration profile. The Student is not a party to this Agreement.

Effective: The date and time the Parent completes the clickwrap registration at tapnpayplus.com

Recitals

WHEREAS TapNPay+, a registered trade name (DBA) of IntrActvMrktg, LLC, operates a consumer cash back membership rewards platform that sells membership tiers to the public, enabling members to earn cash back on Digital Gift Card purchases from major retailers.

WHEREAS TapNPay+ operates the HS Parents program through which parents and legal guardians of K-12 students enroll as promoters, share the TapNPay+ platform with their community, and earn cash back compensation based on their documented promotional activity and resulting community membership activations.

WHEREAS TapNPay+ is a family consumer cash back membership program. It is not a Name, Image, and Likeness (NIL) program. The Student named in the Parent's registration profile is the beneficiary of the Parent's earnings under this Agreement. The Student is not a party to this Agreement, does not earn compensation, does not promote the program, and has no contractual relationship with TapNPay+ under this enrollment.

Section 1 — Definitions

Key definitions include: TapNPay+ Platform, HS Parent Program, Membership ($30-$1,000 tiers), Community Member, Share Activation (documented promotional act via in-app Share button), Pre-Loaded Share Message, Digital Gift Card (DGC), Membership Activation Commission (70%), DGC Cash Back Performance Royalty (10%), Active Status (1+ shares per 30-day window), and Paused Status (0 shares = ineligible for compensation).

Section 2 — Not a NIL Program — Critical Clarification

The HS Parents Program is not a Name, Image, and Likeness (NIL) program in any respect. TapNPay+ does not use the Student's name, image, or likeness. The Student earns no compensation and has no promotional obligations. The Parent is the sole promoter. The Student is the beneficiary only. No school approval is required.

Section 3 — Parent Eligibility and Enrollment

Parent must be at least 18 years of age. Parent enrolls as sole account holder. Student must be enrolled in a K-12 school or equivalent. Parent represents all registration information is accurate.

Section 4 — Promotional Obligations

Parent's primary obligation is to perform Share Activations using the in-app Share button. Minimum one (1) Share Activation per rolling 30-day period maintains Active Status. The Pre-Loaded Share Message may not be modified. Additional promotional content is voluntary but must include #ad or #TapNPayPartner disclosure.

Section 5 — Compensation

Membership Activation Commission — 70%: Parent earns 70% of each Membership purchase price ($30=$21, $100=$70, $250=$175, $500=$350, $1,000=$700).

DGC Cash Back Performance Royalty — 10%: Parent earns 10% of all cash back earned by their attributed Community Members on Digital Gift Card purchases. No compensation is tied to Student performance. No compensation during Paused Status.

Payout requires W-9 verification. Form 1099 issued at $600+ annual earnings. Parent is responsible for tax reporting as independent contractor.

Section 6 — Community Member Value Proposition

Every Community Member receives full access to 300+ national brand partners with an average of 4% unlimited instant cash back on Digital Gift Card purchases, plus 24/7 customer support.
